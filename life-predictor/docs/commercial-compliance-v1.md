# LifeScore Commercial Compliance v1

Last updated: 2026-06-04

This is a practical launch checklist for turning LifeScore into a real public product. It is product guidance, not legal advice.

## Public Pages Added

- `/privacy`: privacy policy for questionnaire data, local drafts, server storage, AI use, ads, and user choices.
- `/disclaimer`: medical disclaimer and safety boundaries.
- `/advertising`: recommendation and advertising disclosure.

## Product Rules We Should Keep

- Health results are educational and probabilistic, not diagnosis or treatment.
- Do not tell users to stop, start, or change medication.
- Emergency symptoms must point users to local emergency services.
- Sponsored or partner recommendations must be clearly labeled.
- Do not use sensitive health answers for targeted advertising unless there is explicit consent and a reviewed legal basis.
- Do not sell individual health questionnaire answers to advertisers.

## Before Public Launch

- Replace placeholder contact language with a real support/privacy email.
- Decide data retention period and deletion process.
- Confirm hosting logs, analytics, AI provider, error monitoring, and ad vendors; list them in the privacy policy.
- Add consent handling if using analytics or ad tech that requires consent.
- Add an internal process for user deletion requests.
- Add final company/operator name, registered address if needed, and governing law language.
- Review the pages with a lawyer for the target launch markets.

## Advertising Rollout

Phase 1: reserved recommendation slots only.

- Use neutral copy such as "Health tools" or "Partner recommendation".
- Do not load third-party ad scripts yet.
- Do not personalize recommendations from sensitive answers.

Phase 2: labeled sponsorships.

- Add "Sponsored" or "Partner" labels.
- Keep recommendations related to broad categories: checkups, movement, sleep, nutrition, education.
- Avoid claims about curing disease, guaranteed longevity, or medical outcomes.

Phase 3: ad network or affiliate links.

- Review the ad network's health, personalized advertising, and sensitive-interest policies.
- Add consent management where required.
- Update privacy policy and advertising disclosure before launch.
